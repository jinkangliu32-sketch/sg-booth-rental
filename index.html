FAQ

Common vendor questions

What information should I send first?

Send your product category, preferred dates, booth size, setup needs, and contact number.

Can new vendors apply?

Yes. New vendors can send product details and photos so we can suggest a suitable booth format.

Will the form send automatically?

The form opens WhatsApp with your enquiry text. You still need to press send inside WhatsApp.

Can I book more than one date?

Yes. Include your preferred month or date range in the enquiry so available options can be checked.
